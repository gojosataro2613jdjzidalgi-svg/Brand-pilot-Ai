BrandPilot AI ("BrandPilot AI," "we," "us," or "our") provides an AI-powered content generation tool that helps brands create marketing copy, including advertisements, product descriptions, social media posts, slogans, emails, and video scripts. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using BrandPilot AI, you agree to the practices described below.

1 Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide. This includes your brand name, product or service details, target audience descriptions, marketing goals, tone preferences, and any other text you enter into the content generator.
  • Account information. If you create an account, we may collect your name, email address, and password (stored in encrypted form).
  • Usage data. We automatically collect information about how you use BrandPilot AI, such as the features you access, the content types you generate, and timestamps of your activity.
  • Device and technical data. This includes your IP address, browser type, operating system, and general location derived from your IP address.
  • Cookies and similar technologies. See the "Cookies" section below for details.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information (such as health, financial, or biometric data) through the content generator, and we ask that you avoid entering such information into your prompts.

2 How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to:

  • Generate the marketing content you request and improve the accuracy and relevance of that content;
  • Operate, maintain, and improve BrandPilot AI's features and performance;
  • Personalize your experience, including remembering your preferred language, tone, and platform settings;
  • Communicate with you about updates, security notices, and support requests;
  • Monitor for misuse, fraud, or violations of our terms of service;
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our agreements.

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with trusted service providers (such as cloud hosting and AI model providers) solely to operate BrandPilot AI, under contractual confidentiality obligations.

3 AI-Generated Content Disclaimer

BrandPilot AI uses artificial intelligence models to draft marketing content based on the details you provide. Please note:

  • AI-generated content is a starting point, not a finished, verified product. It may contain inaccuracies, unintended phrasing, or claims that require fact-checking.
  • You are responsible for reviewing, editing, and approving all generated content before publishing or distributing it, including checking for factual accuracy, legal compliance (such as advertising regulations), and brand appropriateness.
  • Generated content may occasionally resemble existing content created by others. We recommend reviewing output for originality before public use.
  • We do not guarantee that generated content will achieve any particular marketing outcome, such as increased sales or engagement.
Tip: Always treat AI output as a first draft. Review it the same way you would review copy from a junior team member before it goes live.

4 Cookies

BrandPilot AI uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Essential cookies — keep you signed in and remember your form inputs and settings during a session;
  • Preference cookies — remember your chosen language, tone, and platform defaults for future visits;
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how BrandPilot AI is used so we can improve performance and features.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect how well BrandPilot AI functions.

5 Security

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect your information, including encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS), encrypted storage of sensitive fields such as passwords, restricted employee access on a need-to-know basis, and regular monitoring for unauthorized activity. No online service can guarantee absolute security, so we encourage you to use a strong, unique password and to contact us immediately if you suspect unauthorized access to your account.
